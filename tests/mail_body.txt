Join Our Client as a Business Architect!

As a Business Architect, you will play a crucial role in translating strategic objectives into structured business architecture artefacts.

Your responsibilities will include:

- Developing and maintaining the business capability model for market operations and power systems. 
- Defining target-state business architectures in response to regulatory and market changes. 
- Collaborating with solution and enterprise architects to ensure seamless flow of business requirements. 
- Leading pre-project analysis and developing project concepts with well-defined scope and risk management. 
- Engaging with stakeholders to capture requirements and validating architecture artefacts. 
- Continuously improving processes and optimising the ways of working for the business analyst team. 
- Providing thought leadership and strategic insights based on your expertise.

What We're Looking For:

- A deep understanding of electricity market design and the regulatory landscape. 
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills. 
- Excellent verbal and written communication abilities. 
- Experience operating with agile and waterfall methodologies. 
- A collaborative spirit and the ability to work effectively with diverse teams.
